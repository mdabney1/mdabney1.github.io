Bio

I'm a Scottish-American industiralist, business magnate, and philanthropist. I led the expansion of the American steel industry in the late 19th century and became one of the richest Americans in history. I also became a leading philanthropist in the United States and the British Empire. During the last 18 years of my life, I away about $350 million to charities, foundarions, and universities - almost 90 percent of my fortune. My 1889 article proclaiming "The Gospel of Wealth" called on the rich to use their wealth to improve society, and stimulated a wave of philanthropy.
